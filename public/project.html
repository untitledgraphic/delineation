Peter Bishop Cader Idris

Peter Bishop a highly talented painter specialises in paintings of Cader Idris and the surrounding mountains. His Book Cader Idris was designed to accompany an exhibition at MoMA Wales.

Craig designed an exhibition catalogue for me in 2012. His undoubted talent as a designer is aided by his ability to communicate fully with his client over any issue or concern over the development process culminating in the final publication. The Cader Idris cover is a work of art in itself. As an art world professional I always enjoy showing Craig's superb catalogue design to my collogues and friends.

A careful balance of typography and colour to compliment the artworks yet still let them leap from the page
